BSShaderType

The type of animation interpolation (blending) that will be used on the associated key frames.

Choices

Number Name Description
0 SHADER_TALL_GRASS Tall Grass Shader
1 SHADER_DEFAULT Standard Lighting Shader
10 SHADER_SKY Sky Shader
14 SHADER_SKIN Skin Shader
17 SHADER_WATER Water Shader
29 SHADER_LIGHTING30 Lighting 3.0 Shader
32 SHADER_TILE Tiled Shader
33 SHADER_NOLIGHTING No Lighting Shader
